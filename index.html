Problem We Solve for Teens

We are addressing two major challenges for teenagers. First, we help teens discover their passions early to prevent a Quarter-Life Crisis, a problem faced by 75% of adults, according to LinkedIn, with 60% unsure about finding a fulfilling career. Second, we tackle daily issues such as tech distractions, mental health, and relationships, noting that 1 in 6 teens suffer from mental health issues, as reported by India Today.

  • Long term Problem - Finding their Passion
  • Short term Problem - Tackling Daily Challenges
