Building Foundations: Essential Skills for Teenage Development

We have carefully chosen these essential skills to nurture teenagers in all aspects of their development, preparing them for a well-rounded future.

Academic Life Skills

Creativity and Problem Solving

Enhances confidence and self-esteem by equipping teens to tackle challenges effectively.

Critical thinking and Colloboration

Encourages teens to ask insightful questions in the classroom, fostering deeper understanding and effective teamwork.

Metacognition and Focus Enhancement

Improves attentiveness and discipline in the classroom, leading to better academic performance.

Efective use of Technology and Internet

Promotes out-of-the-box thinking and innovative problem-solving.

Time Management

Develops the ability to prioritize tasks efficiently, enhancing productivity and reducing stress.

Personal Development Life Skills

Mental Health and Stress Management

Cultivates resilience to overcome personal challenges and insecurities during teenage years.

Emotional Intelligence and Communication

Fosters healthy friendships and relationships through improved understanding and expression of emotions.

Environment and Respecting Diversity

Cultivates positive relationships with classmates from diverse backgrounds while promoting cleanliness and hygiene improvements.

Health and Wellness

Encourages good dietary habits and raises awareness about harmful substances like drugs, fostering overall well-being.

Financial Literacy

Improves management of pocket money and provides foundational knowledge in finance.
